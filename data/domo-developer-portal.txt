Community
Domo has a vibrant community that creates extraordinarily useful content. This content is not maintained or officially endorsed by Domo, but it is still valuable. Here are some notable resources:
Domo User Group Slack Community: An active Slack community of Domo users sharing knowledge, tips, and best practices. Even some Domo employees, like Ben Schein, participate here.
Dashboard Dudes by John Le: Courses, faqs / tips, templates, and consulting.
DataCrew by Jae Wilson: Community space with resources. See his YouTube Channel for many great videos.
Ellibot YouTube Channel by Elliott Leonard.
Mark on Domo YouTube Channel by Mark Snodgrass.
Product APIs Postman Collection by Bryce Cindrich: A Postman collection with examples for many of Domo's API endpoints. Also check out his GitHub for useful scripts using the Product APIs.
